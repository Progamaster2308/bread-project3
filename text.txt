## Hoja 1: Comprensión de Lectura y Vocabulario

### Vocabulario Inicial

**Which words mean an item for sale is in very good condition? (Choose three)**
Las tres opciones correctas son:

* **A.** immaculate condition
* **B.** no wear and tear
* **D.** unmarked

### Task 1: Are the sentences true or false?

Basado en el texto "SOFA FOR SALE!!!!!!!":

1. **Short adverts with the key details work best.**
* **True** (Es una regla general de los anuncios, y el ejemplo es directo).


2. **Don’t give information about the condition of the item.**
* **False** (El texto menciona específicamente que está en "immaculate condition").


3. **Use clear paragraphs.**
* **True** (El anuncio utiliza párrafos separados para la descripción, el estado y las medidas).


4. **You mustn’t use bullet points.**
* **False** (El anuncio utiliza puntos/bullets para las medidas).


5. **Delivery information isn’t necessary.**
* **False** (El anuncio especifica claramente "**For collection ONLY**").


6. **You must always write in complete sentences.**
* **False** (En los anuncios se suelen usar frases cortas o fragmentos para ser directos).

## Hoja 2: Ejercicios de Escritura

### Task 2: Write the words that have been left out

Debes completar las oraciones con el sujeto (usualmente "It is" o "It has") que se omitió en el estilo de anuncio:

1. **It is a** beautiful leather sofa in excellent condition.
2. **It is in** immaculate condition.
3. **It** keeps its shape when you wash it.
4. **There is** no wear and tear.
5. **It comes** from a smoke-free house.
6. **I am** only selling due to lack of space.

### Task 3: Complete the advert

Usa las palabras del recuadro (**Nearest, design, due to, Square, immediate, wear and tear, collection, Perfect**) para completar el espacio:

Beautiful, large, white coffee table for **(1) sale**. Good condition. Sleek, modern **(2) design**, with a solid wood base and a glass top. **(3) Square** shape, 120 x 120 x 50 cm. **(4) Perfect** for books, magazines, drinks or plates of snacks. Minor **(5) wear and tear**, not really noticeable. Selling **(6) due to** moving house. Please call or message Alex at 09876 543210 to view. Asking price £50 or **(7) nearest** offer. For **(8) collection** only.

## Discussion & Writing Task

### Discussion

**Is buying or selling second-hand things online a good way of shopping? EXPLAIN WHY**
*Respuesta sugerida:* Yes, it is a great way to shop because it is more sustainable for the planet. It allows people to reuse items instead of throwing them away. Also, it is much cheaper than buying new things, which helps you save money.

### Writing Task: Your Own Advert

*Instrucción: Escribe un anuncio de al menos 150 palabras (aquí tienes un ejemplo de una bicicleta).*

**MOUNTAIN BIKE FOR SALE - EXCELLENT CONDITION!**

I am selling my professional mountain bike because I recently upgraded to an electric model. This bike is a "TrailMaster 3000" with a lightweight aluminum frame and 21-speed Shimano gears. It is in **immaculate condition** as it has only been used a handful of times on paved park paths. There is absolutely **no wear and tear** on the tires or the seat.

**Key Features:**

* **Material:** Lightweight alloy frame.
* **Size:** Medium (suitable for heights 165 cm - 180 cm).
* **Extras:** Includes a front LED light, a water bottle holder, and a sturdy kickstand.
* **Color:** Matte Black with orange details.

It has been kept in a dry garage, so there is no rust. The brakes were serviced last month and work perfectly. This is a **perfect** bike for someone looking to start cycling or for a daily commute to work.

**Price:** £180 or nearest offer.
**Location:** Central area. **For collection ONLY.**
Please send a direct message if you want to arrange a viewing this weekend. **Immediate** sale preferred. Don't miss out on this bargain!

---

*Nota: El texto del anuncio tiene aproximadamente 170 palabras, cumpliendo con el requisito de "al menos 150".*
